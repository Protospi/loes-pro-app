**AI Hotel Concierge with Multi-Agent Orchestration** *Built at SmartTalks.ai*

Developed an intelligent hotel concierge system with multi-agent architecture, managing guest services from pre-arrival to post-departure including room service, check-in, loyalty programs, and local tourism guidance.

**Core Capabilities:**

* Multi-agent orchestration system with intelligent routing to specialized sub-agents:  
  * **FAQ & Hotel Information Agent**: Hotel rules, services, and general inquiries  
  * **Room Service Agent**: Food and drink ordering with menu presentation (text and images)  
  * **Fidelity Program Agent**: Points management, redemption, and rewards  
  * **Tourism Guide Agent**: Local recommendations and city information  
* Guest recognition and personalization by name or CPF  
* Context-aware responses using current date/time and guest preferences  
* Automated conversation summarization dynamically injected into system prompts, enabling seamless context retention across multiple sessions  
* Multilanguage support for international client engagement

**Guest Services:**

* Digital check-in process integrated with hotel management system  
* Room service ordering with visual menu presentation, itemized checkout summary with pricing, and direct kitchen integration  
* Guest reservation lookup and modification capabilities  
* Loyalty program management: point registration, balance tracking, and redemption for perks (late checkout, discounts, bar items, etc.)

**Intelligence Layer:**

* RAG system for hotel information, services, policies, and FAQs  
* Comprehensive menu database with pricing and visual assets  
* Guest history and preference tracking

**Tourism & Local Information:**

* **External API Integration:**  
  * Google Places API for points of interest (tourist attractions, business centers, restaurants)  
  * Google Maps Routes API for directions and travel time estimates  
  * Weather API for current conditions and forecasts  
* Personalized recommendations based on guest interests (tourism, business, dining)  
* Operating hours and real-time information for local establishments

**Proactive Engagement:**

* Pre-arrival communication and welcome messaging  
* Post-stay follow-up for feedback and future booking opportunities  
* Active campaign integration for guest retention and upselling

**Support & Escalation:**

* Escalation protocol to hotel staff when needed  
* Comprehensive data storage (conversation logs, guest history, order records, summarized conversations, tool calls, system errors, CSAT scores)

**Business Intelligence:**

* Analytics dashboard tracking usage metrics, costs, guest satisfaction, service utilization, and conversion rates  
* Error logging and learning flags to identify opportunities for improving agent prompts, tools, and integrations  
* Guest behavior insights and revenue optimization tracking

**Technology Stack:**

* Frontend: Vue.js  
* Backend: Node.js, JavaScript  
* Database: MongoDB  
* External APIs: Google Places, Google Maps Routes, Weather API

**Impact:** Comprehensive guest experience platform combining multi-agent orchestration, hotel operations integration, loyalty management, and local intelligence to deliver personalized concierge services and drive revenue within the SmartTalks.ai ecosystem.
