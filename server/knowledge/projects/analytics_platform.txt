**AI-Powered Analytics & Monitoring Dashboard** *Built at SmartTalks.ai*

Developed a comprehensive analytics platform combining AI-driven conversation analysis with real-time performance monitoring, providing actionable insights for conversational agent optimization and human support team management across multiple specialized dashboards.

**AI Conversation Analytics with Copilot:**

* **Semantic conversation analysis agent** evaluating dialogue quality and effectiveness  
* **AI Copilot assistant** helping users navigate data and discover insights through natural language queries  
* Dynamic prompt generation allowing users to specify custom analysis dimensions and metrics  
* **On-the-fly dashboard creation** tailored to specific business questions  
* Quality assurance monitoring for agent behavior and error detection  
* Performance improvement recommendations based on conversation patterns

**Dashboard Suite:**

**1\. General Metrics Dashboard:**

* **Core KPI Tracking:**  
  * Total users, messages, and conversations  
  * Channel performance breakdown  
  * Client and business unit segmentation  
* **Advanced Visualizations:**  
  * Time series charts with multiple granularity options (hourly, daily, weekly, monthly)  
  * Metric combination capabilities for comparative analysis  
  * Geographic heat maps tracking user distribution by location  
  * Word clouds highlighting frequent topics and keywords  
* Flexible date range filtering and period comparison

**2\. Human Attendance Dashboard:**

* **Support Team Performance Monitoring:**  
  * Ticket status tracking (open, in-progress, resolved, closed)  
  * Response time metrics (first response, average resolution time)  
  * Success and failure rate analysis  
  * Multi-dimensional filtering by department, unit, date, and individual attendant  
* **Workload Optimization:**  
  * Heat map visualization showing peak hours and days of the week  
  * Request concentration patterns for team capacity planning  
  * Resource allocation insights

**3\. AI Agent Analytics Dashboard:**

* Powered by semantic analysis AI  
* Agent behavior monitoring and quality metrics  
* Error detection and improvement opportunities  
* Conversation flow analysis  
* Custom dimension exploration via AI Copilot

**4\. Sales Dashboard:**

* **Revenue Metrics:**  
  * Total sales value (in dollars)  
  * User journey and sales funnel visualization  
  * Conversion rates at each funnel stage  
  * Lead qualification and progression tracking  
* CSAT and NPS scores  
* Sales performance trends

**5\. Scheduling Dashboard:**

* Total appointments scheduled  
* Appointment status tracking  
* Booking patterns and trends  
* Scheduling conversion metrics  
* Calendar utilization analysis

**6\. Admin Dashboard:**

* **Multi-tenant management** for platform oversight  
* Customer account tracking and health metrics  
* Cross-client performance comparison  
* Platform usage statistics  
* Tenant activity monitoring

**7\. Cost Dashboard:**

* **LLM Usage & Cost Tracking:**  
  * Token consumption by model  
  * Cost breakdown per agent, client, and conversation  
  * Budget monitoring and forecasting  
  * Model performance vs. cost analysis  
  * Usage optimization recommendations

**Comprehensive Business Intelligence:**

* **360-degree view** addressing all business angles:  
  * Operational efficiency (general metrics)  
  * Customer experience (CSAT, NPS, conversation quality)  
  * Revenue generation (sales funnel, conversion rates)  
  * Resource optimization (human attendance, scheduling)  
  * Cost management (LLM usage)  
  * Platform health (admin overview)  
* Real-time data updates across all dashboards  
* Export capabilities for reporting and further analysis

**Technology Stack:**

* Frontend: Vue.js  
* Backend: Node.js, JavaScript  
* Database: MongoDB

**Impact:** Enterprise-grade analytics ecosystem combining AI-powered semantic analysis, natural language copilot, and specialized dashboards delivering comprehensive insights across operations, sales, support, and cost management within the SmartTalks.ai platform.
