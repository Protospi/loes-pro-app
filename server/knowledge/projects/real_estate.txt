**AI Sales Agent for Real Estate Apartments** *Built at SmartTalks.ai*

Developed an intelligent sales agent for a real estate company, managing property consultations, lead qualification, and customer journey tracking with comprehensive property database integration.

**Core Capabilities:**

* Specialized conversational AI for real estate consultation and apartment sales  
* Real-time integration with company property database retrieving:  
  * Property pricing and availability  
  * Comprehensive feature lists (apartment and building amenities)  
  * Location details and address information  
  * Exclusions and limitations  
* Visual property presentation with hundreds of high-quality apartment images  
* Multilanguage support for international client engagement  
* Context-aware responses using current date/time and market conditions  
* Automated conversation summarization dynamically injected into system prompts, enabling seamless context retention across multiple sessions

**Intelligence Layer:**

* RAG system for frequently asked questions covering:  
  * Real estate law and regulations  
  * Contract terms and conditions  
  * Financing options and mortgage information  
  * Purchase process guidance  
* Lead qualification through preference capture and requirement gathering

**CRM Integration:**

* Customer information registration and updates in real estate database  
* Property preference profiling for human consultant handoff  
* Lead tracking through sales journey funnel stages  
* Comprehensive client requirement documentation

**Proactive Engagement:**

* Active campaign integration for targeted property offers  
* Lead nurturing based on expressed preferences and budget  
* Follow-up automation for interested prospects

**Support & Escalation:**

* Escalation protocol to human real estate consultants when needed  
* Seamless handoff with complete client preference profile and conversation context  
* Comprehensive data storage (conversation logs, client preferences, property views, tool calls, system errors, lead status)

**Business Intelligence:**

* Analytics dashboard tracking usage metrics, costs, customer satisfaction, lead geography, and sales funnel progression  
* Error logging and learning flags to identify opportunities for improving agent prompts, tools, and integrations  
* Property interest analytics and conversion tracking  
* Lead quality scoring and sales performance metrics

**Technology Stack:**

* Frontend: Vue.js  
* Backend: Node.js, JavaScript  
* Database: MongoDB

**Impact:** End-to-end real estate sales solution combining property showcase, intelligent consultation, lead qualification, and CRM integration to optimize the apartment sales process and improve conversion rates within the SmartTalks.ai ecosystem.
